## Reading This Table

- ✅ = favorable signal for a prospective buyer
- ⚠️ = neutral or requires further investigation
- 🔴 = unfavorable signal; buyer should investigate before proceeding
- **Bold values** highlight notable outliers within each row
- Blank cells or dashes indicate the brand does not report that metric
- All data from 2025 FDDs (reporting on FY2024). Single-year snapshot.

**This table is a decision-support tool, not a ranking.** No single row determines
whether a franchise is a good or bad investment. A buyer's specific market,
financial resources, risk tolerance, and operational goals should drive the
final decision.
