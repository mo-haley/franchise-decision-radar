Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

What we collect

Franchise Decision Radar is a static website. We do not require account creation, do not store passwords, and do not maintain a user database.

We collect limited data through the following:

  • Google Analytics — anonymous page-view and interaction data (pages visited, time on site, referral source, device type). We use this to understand which content is useful and improve the site. No personally identifiable information is collected through Analytics.
  • Stripe — when you purchase a Decision Report, payment is processed by Stripe. Stripe collects your payment information (card number, billing address, email) under their own privacy policy. We receive your email and purchase confirmation from Stripe but do not store or see your full card details.

How we use your information

  • To deliver the report you purchased
  • To respond if you contact us with a support request
  • To understand aggregate site usage and improve content

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

Cookies

Google Analytics sets cookies to distinguish unique visitors and sessions. Stripe may set cookies during the payment process. We do not set any first-party tracking cookies beyond what these services require.

Your rights

If you would like to know what data we hold about you, request deletion, or have any privacy-related questions, contact us at support@franchisedecisionradar.com.

Changes

We may update this policy as the site evolves. Material changes will be noted with an updated date at the top of this page.
