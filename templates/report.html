What’s inside

Decision Scorecard

Rated assessment across 8 dimensions — entry cost, ongoing fees, system stability, revenue disclosure, downside risk, buyer fit, and overall.

Fee Modeling & Comparison

Total ongoing cost at 4 revenue levels with component breakdown. Visual comparison against direct competitors. Hidden fees, escalation triggers, conditional minimums.

Item 19 Translation

What the financial performance data tells you — and what it omits. Exclusion rates, population definitions, and what reported averages mean for a new single-territory operator.

Economics & Payback Sensitivity

Illustrative scenarios at different operating levels. Payback timelines under conservative, moderate, and strong assumptions. A framework, not a forecast.

System Health & Risk Signals

Unit growth trends, termination patterns, transfer activity, litigation outcomes, and cross-brand context a single-brand attorney review wouldn’t have.

Discovery Day Diligence Script

8–10 questions derived from this brand’s actual FDD — with context on why each matters, what a strong answer sounds like, and what evasion sounds like.

Also includes: buyer-fit profile, investment breakdown, peer positioning with decision overlay. Every claim traceable to a specific FDD page.
