Source & Methodology

Source document: {{ brand_name }} 2025 Franchise Disclosure Document, filed with the Wisconsin Department of Financial Institutions. {{ page_count }}-page regulator-source PDF. SHA-256: {{ sha256 }}

Extraction: Structured data extraction using pdfplumber with manual review. Field-level provenance recorded (source year, state, page number, item reference). Extraction confidence: high (3+ fields spot-checked against source).

Fee modeling: Year 5 assumptions, single territory, 1 owner + 1 technician. Revenue distributed evenly across the year. All mandatory fees enforced. See fee_modeling_framework.md for full methodology.

Economics scenarios: Revenue estimates drawn from Item 19 data. COGS ratio is brand-specific (derived from available company-owned P&L data; see Economics section for derivation and assumptions). Scenarios are illustrative, not predictive. Operating costs beyond franchisor fees and COGS are not estimated — the buyer must validate these independently.

Peer comparison: Same methodology applied to all 5 brands in cohort (Mosquito Authority, Mosquito Hunters, Mosquito Joe, Mosquito Shield, Mosquito Squad). All from 2025 FDDs filed with Wisconsin DFI.
