Report delivery issues

Decision Reports are delivered as an instant PDF download immediately after purchase. If you completed payment but did not receive your download link, check your browser’s pop-up blocker and look for the Stripe confirmation email.

If the download still isn’t working, email us at support@franchisedecisionradar.com with your name and the brand you purchased. We’ll send the PDF directly.

Data corrections

Every data point on this site traces to a specific page in a regulator-filed FDD. If you believe a number is incorrect, let us know the brand, the data point, and what you believe the correct value is. We’ll verify against the source document and correct any errors.
